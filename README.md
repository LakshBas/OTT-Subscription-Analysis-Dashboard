#Overview:

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze OTT platform subscriptions, user behavior, and content preferences across major streaming services like Netflix, Amazon Prime, ZEE5, Disney+ Hotstar, and SonyLIV. The project aimed to derive insights into user engagement, subscription trends, viewing habits by age group, and the impact of plan tiers (Basic, Standard, Premium) on platform performance.

#Key Responsibilities

->Collected, cleaned, and structured dummy OTT subscription data.
->Designed a multi-layered Power BI dashboard using filters, slicers, and visual bookmarks.
->Built insightful visualizations: pie charts, donut charts, line graphs, stacked bars, and KPI cards.
->Implemented custom color themes and plan-tier logic for clear user segmentation.
->Conducted analysis on genre preference by age group, plan type vs. platform, and monthly revenue trends.

#Skills & Tools: 

Power BI, DAX, Power Query, Excel

#Key Highlights from Analysis:

->Amazon Prime and Disney+ Hotstar contributed equally to the total subscriptions (21.43% each).
->Standard plan was the most preferred tier (40%), followed by Basic and Premium (30% each).
->Genre viewing patterns varied significantly across age groups:
18–24 preferred Action & Comedy, while
55+ favored Documentary & Drama.
->Netflix had the lowest monthly cost but still had a strong user base.
->Total estimated monthly revenue was ₹23K with 1.88K total viewing hours and 50 total subscriptions.
